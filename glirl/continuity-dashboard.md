GLIRL Continuity Dashboard (v0.1)

Real‑Time Monitoring of Identity, Narrative, and Structural Continuity



1\. Purpose of the Continuity Dashboard

The Continuity Dashboard is the civilisation’s continuity command centre.



It provides:



real‑time continuity status



long‑horizon projections



invariant health



narrative coherence indicators



figment constellation dynamics



cross‑layer alignment



continuity break alerts



repair status



It answers:



Is the civilisation structurally coherent right now?



And:



Where is continuity trending next?



2\. Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is composed of six panels:



Identity Panel



Figment Panel



Action Panel



Narrative Panel



Invariant Panel



Cross‑Layer Panel



Each panel displays continuity metrics, graphs, and alerts.



3\. Identity Panel

The Identity Panel visualises:



identity lineage graph



current identity state



transformation coherence



lineage depth



lineage stability



pending redeclarations



Key Indicators

Identity Continuity Score



Lineage Stability Index



Redeclaration Eligibility



Identity Drift Warning



Visual Elements

hexagon nodes (identity states)



solid edges (identity continuity)



colour coding for stability

9\. Continuity Health Bar

A single composite metric:



Code
