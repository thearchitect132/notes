1\. Purpose of the Continuity Lab

The Continuity Lab is the civilisation’s continuity research environment.



It allows you to:



experiment with identity evolution



manipulate figment constellations



test action derivation



explore narrative arcs



stress‑test invariants



simulate cross‑layer alignment



visualise continuity graphs



trigger and repair continuity breaks



run long‑horizon projections



It answers:



What happens to continuity when we experiment with the structure of becoming?



The Lab is where continuity becomes interactive.
