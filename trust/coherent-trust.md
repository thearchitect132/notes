# Coherent‑Trust (CT)

Coherent‑Trust is the **final stage** of the trust pipeline.  
It is not blind trust, not authority‑based trust, and not inherited trust.  
It is **trust that emerges from consistent, verifiable behaviour**.

Coherent‑Trust is the epistemic equilibrium of the civilisation‑stack.

---

## Core Principles

- **Claims match reality**  
- **Outputs match expectations**  
- **Behaviour is stable across time**  
- **The system is inspectable and predictable**  
- **Trust is earned, not assumed**  

Coherent‑Trust is the point where the system becomes **reliable by virtue of coherence**.

---

## How Coherent‑Trust Emerges

Coherent‑Trust cannot be manufactured.  
It **emerges** when:

- Zero‑Trust removes assumptions  
- Zero‑Knowledge Architecture provides cryptographic guarantees  
- The system behaves consistently within that envelope  

Coherence is the *result* of the pipeline, not the beginning.
